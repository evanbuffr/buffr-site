About Buffr

Buffr is more than a payment or banking app. We're "Your Next Payment Companion," enhancing financial inclusion with modern, futuristic payments. Our innovative approach ensures financial services are accessible, affordable, and enjoyable.

Our Purpose

Empowering users with personalized financial services and seamless, secure, innovative payment solutions.

Our Vision

To empower individuals and businesses in Southern Africa by making financial services accessible, affordable, and enjoyable.

Our Features

AI-Powered Financial Advisor

Unlock your financial potential with Buffr's intuitive AI-powered financial advisor. Get personalized budgeting and savings recommendations based on your data and financial goals.

Unified Dashboard

Manage multiple bank accounts and mobile money wallets in one place with a seamless payment experience across financial platforms.

Buffr Points Rewards

Make payments easy and enjoyable with Buffr. Earn Buffr Points for everyday transactions and redeem them for exciting rewards and exclusive offers.

AI Credit Scoring

Build your credit score and access financial opportunities with our AI-powered credit scoring engine, enabling access for individuals without traditional credit history.

USSD & QR Code Payments

Use USSD codes or scan QR codes to send and receive money instantly, ensuring a secure payment experience even without internet access.

Personalized Loans

Access personalized loan options tailored to your needs with ease and transparency through our AI-powered system.

Our Impact

At Buffr, we're committed to driving positive change in Southern Africa's financial landscape. By providing accessible, innovative financial solutions, we're empowering individuals and businesses to take control of their financial futures.

Join us in our mission to create a more inclusive, financially empowered Southern Africa.

The Shandi: Buffr for Enterprise

The Shandi is a SaaS platform designed to empower restaurants with advanced business intelligence tools, seamless table and menu management, and comprehensive user profile management.

Advanced Table Management

Streamline your restaurant's seating arrangements and maximize efficiency with our intuitive table management system.

Comprehensive Menu Management

Easily update and manage your restaurant's menu with quick changes to menu items and pricing.

Business Intelligence Tools

Make informed decisions with our powerful business intelligence tools to drive growth and profitability.

User Profile Management

Manage customer profiles to enhance satisfaction and encourage repeat business.

Pilot Launch

The Shandi will soon be piloted at Etuna Guesthouse & Tours and Katz Lounge Namibia, demonstrating its effectiveness in real-world restaurant settings.
